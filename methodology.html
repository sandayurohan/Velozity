Our Methodology

Velozity Global Work Methodology includes the steps that will be the tools we will be using to organize the whole project work and not scattered around not knowing where are the start, end, and middle. We make ourselves and Client 100% aware about the best quality output by providing the well-drafted prototype and keeps ourselves involved and keep suggesting the best way to the client so that he/she gets 100% out of his Project in terms of user response and ROR. We provide the best quality output and complete the task in the stipulated timeline and within the budget that we have decided with the Client at the start of the project.

  • Requirement Analysis

    We begin with analyzing customer’s business needs to determine the scope of work and provides a time frame to the customer. During the brief analysis we used to check each and every possibility of design and development by applying our object-oriented methodologies, easy navigation structure, lightweight designs, and many more criteria.

  • Designing

    Once we have a plan what we need to do we start with design and development of the project at the same time we start updating customer so they can verify they’re ongoing work and put their suggestions and inputs on a regular interval. By this process, the customer will have transparency that how much work is done and they can estimate how many resources are allocated on their work.

  • Development

    As soon as we determine requirement and scope of work we devise scope of work into milestones and further into deliverable, so our customer always aware with deliverable to check the progress of their project.

  • Testing

    We understand that testing is a very important part of any project and a major part of the budget needs to be allocated to this phase. No one likes to fail their application once it is published and people started using it whether it is an application project or website development project.

    Our test cases – Functionality, devices compatibility, browser compatibility and many more in technical aspects of any application. We do our best to make sure that application developed on our premises will not let your dreams down.

  • Launch

    We understand the value of projects so each and every process of project development till deployment is done under the guidance of senior people. So after development, testing and bug resolutions is completed with client satisfaction we start deployment process very carefully with the backup of your old data on your server and observes migration process very carefully so that there will not be the chance for any issue related to the server.

  • Maintenance

    We are not done yet although we are done with the deployment process we always stand with the customer for any support or help you want for your project. We are still open for maintenance and enhancement services like a helping hand to keep you growing.

    At Velozity Global, we always keep in mind easy navigation system and user-friendly structure on behalf of you. Our friendly team is always with you for any challenge in your project and feels happy to see you keep smiling with great satisfaction

Key Process of Methodology
  • The client sends an inquiry to the Biz team
  • Biz team sends details to Tech team for analysis
  • Tech team suggests
  • Fixed cost price or
  • Time & Material Model to biz team
  • Biz team does an agreement with the client
  • Biz team sends Project Manager Details / Developer details to client
  • Project Manager / Developer will communicate with the client for the project
